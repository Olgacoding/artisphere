Location

123 Art Lane, Aurora Springs, USA

Parking

Visitor parking is available in the adjacent lot at 123 Art Lane. Rates are $5 per hour or $15 for the day. Street parking is also available on Creative Avenue and surrounding streets.

Public Transport

  • Bus:

    Routes 12, 24, and 36 stop directly in front of the museum at the Creative Avenue stop.

  • Subway:

    The Blue Line to Arts District Station, just a 5-minute walk from the museum.

  • Bike:

    Bike racks are available near the main entrance for those arriving by bicycle.

Amenities

  • Café:

    Enjoy a selection of beverages, snacks, and light meals at our café, located on the ground floor.

  • Gift Shop:

    Browse a curated selection of art books, prints, and souvenirs in our gift shop, also located on the ground floor.

  • Wi-Fi:

    Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the museum.
